The LYFeX Long-Term Internship is designed as a mutually beneficial initiative based on fundamental principles of survival and growth for both startups and students. This program aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry needs by aligning trained, pre-trained, or interested students with companies requiring specific skills. It ensures a win-win scenario for all stakeholders, providing real-world skill application opportunity for students and valuable resources for startups to grow. The program is based on the principle of consistent learning through application.
How LYFeX Long-Term Internship Works

Company Matching

Students from a specific domain are matched with companies based on their requirements.

Orientation

A 2-day orientation session will equip students with essential skills, including professional behavior, expectations, and responsibilities during the internship.

Internship Types

Students can choose between mentorship (guided learning and feedback) or no mentorship (independent hands-on experience).

With Mentorship

Students gain guided training, feedback, and skill-building under an experienced mentor while working for company.

Without Mentorship

Students work independently for the company, earning a stipend while gaining hands-on experience.

Reporting

A biannual report will be shared with students, parents, and schools, providing insights into progress. The program does not involve grading but includes an expertise analysis report, which will be detailed in the annual certificate provided to students.

Flexibility and Replacements

Both students and companies are allowed up to two replacements per year to address any issues that arise during the internship.

Certificate

A yearly certificate is awarded, summarizing the student’s participation and achievements.

About LYFeX

LYFeX (Live Your Future Experience) is an innovative program designed to empower students by providing them with early exposure to real-world careers and professional environments. By blending hands-on experiences, mentorship, and emotional development, LYFeX equips young minds to make informed career decisions and develop essential skills for future success.
