How LYFeX Long-Term Internship Works

Selection

Students apply for the internship through their schools or directly via LYFeX

Orientation

Selected candidates undergo an orientation program to understand the internship's goals, structure, and expectations

Mentorship

Students are assigned a mentor who provides personalized guidance, career insights, and emotional support throughout the internship

Hands-On Projects

Students participate in real projects contributing to meaningful tasks while learning practical skills

Soft Skills Training

Emphasis on emotional intelligence, ethics, and interpersonal skills

Progress Monitoring

Students' progress is evaluated through project reviews, mentor feedback, and self-assessmentsâ€”interactive sessions to reflect on learning outcomes and set goals for the next phase.

Certification and Recommendations

Upon completion, students receive a certification highlighting their skills and achievements. Outstanding performers may also receive recommendation letters from mentors.

Career Pathway Guidance

Post-internship, LYFeX provides career counseling sessions to help students map out their next steps based on their experiences and aspirations

About LYFeX

LYFeX (Live Your Future Experience) is an innovative program designed to empower students by providing them with early exposure to real-world careers and professional environments. By blending hands-on experiences, mentorship, and emotional development, LYFeX equips young minds to make informed career decisions and develop essential skills for future success.
