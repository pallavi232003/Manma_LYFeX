How Shadowing Program Works

The LYFeX Job Shadowing Program is a transformative learning experience based on the principle of observation-designed to help students explore various career paths, develop cognitive skills, and gain clarity about their future. Through real-world exposure, students observe professionals, learn from their expertise, and discover their true interests in a supportive environment- It's completely self-learning.
How LYFeX Shadowing Works

Enrollment

Students sign up for LYFeX Shadowing Program and provide their career interests through a simple onboarding process.

Career Matchmaking

LYFeX pairs students with experienced professionals in their chosen field based on their interests, skills, and aspirations.

Shadowing Sessions

Students spend time observing and interacting with experts during their daily work either online or in-person to gain insight and make informed decisions.

Reflection & Feedback

After each session in the LYFeX Shadowing Program, students reflect on their experiences and receive feedback from experts. This helps them make informed decisions about their career paths.

Personalized Mentorship

Students engage in one-on-one discussions with mentors to address questions, clarify doubts, and receive guidance on future steps in their career journey.

Certification & Guidance

Upon completing the program, students receive a certificate and a personalized career report outlining their strengths, learnings, and suggested career paths.

About LYFeX

LYFeX (Live Your Future Experience) is an innovative program designed to empower students by providing them with early exposure to real-world careers and professional environments.
